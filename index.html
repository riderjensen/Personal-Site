About Me

I am a junior fullstack application developer looking to increase my skillset. I fell into web development about 4 years ago after completing and associate's degree in History and Political Science. I found that not only was I technically minded but many of my past experiences had me very customer service oriented. Helping people is something that I have always loved to do and web development has allowed me to fulfill this purpose in my life while working with technology that I love.

Outside of work, I like to work on my own personal projects, most of which are featured on my GitHub. Among my favorites is a URL shortner that I built open source and easily customizable. The idea came from a PHP URL shortner that is called YOURLS. I wanted to create something similar that anyone could use and that would be available to the community and community contribution. The most challenging part for me was to figure out how to have global variables in my main file (so users would only have to edit one page to get the application up and running) and also use these variables through out the application.

Besides coding, I love playing video games and playing DND with my friends. I play a lot of RPGs and I have sunk an embarassingly large amount of hours into World of Warcraft. I also enjoy my Xbox One though I usually use it to turn on netflix and have sound playing while I work on things on my laptop.
